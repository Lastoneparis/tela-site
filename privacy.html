Tela Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Tela is a bring-your-own-key (BYOK) AI chat client. We host nothing and we do not collect, store, or transmit your data to our own servers.

What stays on your device

  • API keys — stored only in your device Keychain. They never leave your device except to authenticate directly with the AI provider you choose.
  • Conversations — stored locally on your device. With Premium, they may sync through your own private iCloud (Apple CloudKit); we cannot read them.

Third-party AI providers

When you send a message, it goes directly from your device to the AI provider you selected (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek, Groq, OpenRouter) using your own API key. Before your first message to a provider, Tela asks for your explicit consent and names the provider. Your use of each provider is governed by that provider's own privacy policy and terms. Tela is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any provider.

Data we collect

None. Tela has no analytics servers and no account system.

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are handled by Apple. We receive no payment information.

Children

Tela is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
