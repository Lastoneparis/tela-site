Tela Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Tela is a local-first notes, docs & databases app. We host nothing and we do not collect, store, or transmit your data to our own servers. There are no third-party SDKs, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no accounts.

What stays on your device

  • Your content — spaces, documents, and databases are stored locally on your device.
  • AI features — AI-assisted features (such as summaries) run entirely on-device using Apple's system models where available. Your content is never sent to us or to any third party.

Data we collect

None. Tela has no analytics servers and no account system.

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are handled by Apple. We receive no payment information.

Children

Tela is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
